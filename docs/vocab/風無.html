pua2 mun1風無verbto be boring

我耳識終此与学人極風無。

pai2 lua1 can2 ta ka1 tui2 nui2 cuk2 kit pua2 mun1.

I've heard that this teacher is very boring. / 我听说这位老师很无聊。

彼之互心女極風無。

zap2 a huat1 hia1 sa1 kit pua2 mun1.

nui2 lip zep1 ka1 pua2 mun1 yn2?
