English: Where does your little sister want to go? She wants to go to the toy store.
Chinese: 你妹妹想去哪里？他想去玩具店。
Chinese (pinyin): Nǐ mèimèi xiǎng qù nǎlǐ? Tā xiǎng qù wánjùdiàn.
Pekzep (latin transcription): mua2 ut2 sa1 huok1 hia1 mok1 nan2 hue? zap2 hia1 mok1 tu2 gau2 su1 hue.
Pekzep (hanzi transcription): 汝下女輩心行何処？彼心行遊刀銭処。
