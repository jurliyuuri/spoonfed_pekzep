English: Give the book to whomever wants it.
Chinese: 这本书谁想要就给谁吧。
Chinese (pinyin): zhè běn shū, shéi xiǎng yào, jiù gěi shéi ba. (1st shei = 2nd shei, same person)
Pekzep (latin transcription): tui2 ka1 ak1 ie hia1 guk2 a cuk2 a be1.
Pekzep (hanzi transcription): 与此書於心受之人之全。
